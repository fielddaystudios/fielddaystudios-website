Why a field day

Positioning your business can often be seen as a one-time event, but its success lies far beyond the opening ceremony.

Brand, at its best, isn't a decision or creation on paper, but a framework that makes each employee's work matter.

Field Day is inspired by that elementary school rite of passage, when you'd get grass stains on your sneakers and leave with endless energy from the blue sports drink at the volunteer tent.

We believe that for an organization, bringing a brand to life is a group activity. Everyone wants to get involved, figure out their role and play a part in the relay race. We're here to help all your players know just where to pick up the baton and push the finish line forward for all.

Mental health platform

Gave a mental health platform a crisp, authentic voice that cut through a crowded market, built on the heels of a major acquisition.

FAQ

Ready, set, rollout.

What does “brand enablement” really mean?

Beyond the strategy and the creative, a lot of coordination and collaboration goes into making a brand launch and stick. That means not only understanding what it looks like to “cross the finish line,” but who needs to be tapped in to bring your new or evolved brand to life.

Where does the brand live today across your organization's teams and assets, and how can we make a plan to activate the new brand to both your people and the market? This is the work of brand enablement, and we think it can be a lot of fun.

How do you price projects?

We price on a fixed-fee, tailored basis, after discussing your project and your team's needs. But we're flexible and can work with you to get things off the ground.

How long do engagements typically run?

It depends on the size of your company, launch, and the key events tied to it, as well as the support you may need after launch to keep the brand in motion. For a tech company with 500 enterprise customers, a brand evolution could take between 3-6 months. For a small business launching a new brand, it might take 6-12 weeks. It depends, but regardless of the commitment, we're excited to help, from start to finish.

Do you work with small teams or only larger organizations?

We're drawn to innovators, mission-driven companies, and small businesses making a real impact in their community, but size isn't the deciding factor. Whether you're a five-person team or a five-hundred-person company, we want to support your brand's launch and the work it takes to keep it alive.
